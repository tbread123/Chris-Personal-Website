Experience & activities

Campus leadership, museums, opera, a Harvard lab visit, and a charity portrait drive—summarized from my résumé (translated from the original Chinese PDF).

    Lead the magazine’s overall visual design and typography—cover, type, and graphic elements—and coordinate classmates contributing layouts.   2. Sep 2024 — Present Shenzhen Jinshi Museum

    Social media lead (Instagram)

    Create graphics and write captions that introduce Chinese stone culture and the museum’s collections. Maintain a steady publishing rhythm on Instagram to grow public engagement with the artifacts.

  3. Sep — Nov 2025 Memory Project · Art Club

    Project lead — charity portraits

    Mobilized fifteen students for the international Memory Project nonprofit: hand-painted portraits for children in Armenian orphan care. Served as the liaison among the charity, art-club leaders, and faculty; hosted weekly check-ins until every portrait shipped on time. The team is now waiting for footage of the children receiving their portraits—see the photo set below.

  4. Jun — Jul 2025 Harvard CAMlab

    Visiting learner

    Studied how CAMlab turns 3D scans of cultural objects into immersive visual experiences. Offered feedback to interns and interviewed Professor Chenchen Lü; the interview was edited into a WeChat video that reached roughly 13k views at its peak.

  5. Apr — May 2025 Hong Kong Opera

    Mascot & poster designer

    For the children’s opera Bamboo Rhyme by the Harbour · The Panda’s Tale (竹韻香江·熊貓奇緣), designed the mascot and key art. The work was adopted for tickets, flyers, and on-site posters.
